Interested?

Come check us out for free. We only request that you contact us first through this contact form and we'll tell you a good class time to first attend.

Cost

The club fees are $60/month, due at the beginning of every month. Unlike most athletic clubs and gyms, there is no annual contract. If you decide after a couple months you want to quit, you don’t owe additional fees.

Uniforms

Come with a clean gi or appropriate no-gi uniform, mouthguard and athletic cup. If you're new to jiu-jitsu and don't own a gi, don't let that stop you from coming. We have a couple spares you can borrow until you buy one, and if you wait to talk to one of us before you buy a gi, we can help you make a good purchase.

Disclaimer

BRAZILIAN JIU-JITSU IS DANGEROUS AND YOU PARTICIPATE AT YOUR OWN RISK. No one at Railroad Square Jiu-jitsu is pressured to do anything they don’t feel comfortable doing, and almost all of our students have sat out of some of the drills and sparring at one time or another due to injuries or personal judgement of their physical limitations and potential hazard. This is a full-contact sport and injuries commonly occur. Because of the dangerous nature of BJJ, all participants are expected to make safety their first priority.
